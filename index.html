Pop by the Polls

This is a short research article for the 2020 primaries. It covers four issues currently being discussed, a candidate running for President of the United States, a polling place, and a sample ballot.

Read More

Issues

These are the issues that voters and candidates are discussing this primary season.

Obamacare

Obamacare was the healthcare developed under the Obama and Biden administration. President Trump wishes to repeal it.

Taxes

President Trump wishes to lower taxes on big businesses. On the contrary Vice President Biden wishes to raise taxes on anyone making over $400,000.

Immigration

President Trump wishes to build a border wall while Vice President Biden wishes to allow easier access to America, and even more rights for undocumented immigrants.

Court Packing

The issue of whether Vice President Biden will pack the court if he is elected has arised. This means adding more justices to the Supreme Court.

Candidate

This is a person that is running to be nominated as a general election candidate for President of the United States.

Joe Biden

Vice President Biden is the Democratic nominee in the 2020 election for President of the United States. He served as the Vice President for President Obama, and was a senator for many years before that.

Polling Place

This is a location that voters can visit to cast a ballot for candidates they prefer.

Chapel Hill Bible Church

Chapel Hill Bible Church is one of the polling sites in Chapel Hill. It is open from 6:30 A.M. to 7:30 P.M. on election day. It is located at 260 Erwin Road, Chapel Hill NC.
